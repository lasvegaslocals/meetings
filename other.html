Provides free online access to multiple virtual support groups at various agencies for a variety of issues. E.g. Addiction, Caregiver Support, Depression Bipolar Support Alliance, etc.
* Many of their groups seem to be full and have long waitlists. This may not be the best option if you're able to afford another therapy service.

BetterHelp
ðŸš« No insurance required
Out-of-pocket, cost-based online subscription program for therapy of all types. (Group, one-on-one, text-based, phone-based, video, etc.) They are also flexible with costs if you contact their customer service representatives.

Headspace Meditation App
ðŸš« No insurance required
This is a general meditation app that provides guided audio meditations and videos to help calm, refocus, and relax your mind. This pandemic is nothing like this world has ever seen, so it's understandable to feel unusually overwhelmed or anxious; meditation can help ease those feelings. There is a solid free trial with optional subscription purchase for more content.
Tip: Search online for discounts if you do decide to purchase a subscription.
